Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

Engineering students, MBA students, undergraduate & postgraduate students, freshers, and experienced professionals can all participate in teams of 3-4 members.

Is this an online or offline hackathon?

This is a hybrid hackathon. The initial rounds are conducted online, while the finals will be held offline at a college in Pune, India.

What should I submit for Round 1?

For Round 1, you need to submit a pitch deck explaining your AI agent concept and a 2-minute video demonstration.

Are there any registration fees?

No, participation in the WeSee AI Agents Hackathon is completely free of charge.

What kind of internship opportunities are available?

Top-performing teams will be offered 5-6 month remote internships with WeSee, including a monthly stipend. Exceptional performers may receive full-time job offers.
